1. Discovery & Scoring

Scanners visit groups/hashtags. score_relevance filter posts. Qualifying posts are drafted inline via Gemini.

Data: comment_queue.json (status: pending)

2. Autonomous Approval

comment_approver.py runs periodically. It stamps items as approved if a draft exists, or skipped if the LLM failed.

3. Deployment

comment_poster.py picks up approved items and uses Playwright to perform the actual post on FB/IG.

Log: engagement_log.jsonl

Advanced Orchestration (LangGraph): The system also includes comment_composer_graph.py, which uses LangGraph for a state-machine based approach to comment drafting and posting. It enables complex branching, retries, and human-in-the-loop checkpoints during the engagement flow.

🎯 Brand Funnel Architecture

The dogfoodandfun brand operates a multi-channel inbound marketing and affiliate monetization funnel built around the "Nalla's Dad" persona.

1. Top of Funnel: Awareness & Discovery

Goal: Organic reach and brand presence.

  • Proactive Engagement: Automated agents (fb-scanner, ig-scanner) post helpful, non-salesy replies in Facebook Groups and Instagram hashtags.
  • Content Generation: AI-generated 9:16 Reels published to Instagram and Facebook.
  • Group Publishing: fb-group-publisher distributes WP blog posts into relevant FB groups.

2. Middle of Funnel: Engagement & Traffic Routing

Goal: Drive traffic to owned property (dogfoodandfun.com).

  • First-Comment CTA: Reels prompt users to comment a keyword (e.g., Comment RECIPE), triggering a DM/reply with the full article link.
  • Bio-Link Fallback: 🔗 Full guide: link in bio serves as a secondary path.
  • Traffic Centralization: All channels point directly to the WP site for better attribution and control.

3. Bottom of Funnel: Conversion & Monetization

Goal: Monetize traffic via targeted affiliate campaigns.

  • The Blog: SEO-optimized WP posts act as landing pages.
  • Affiliate Resolution: affiliate_resolver dynamically swaps placeholders (e.g., [AFFILIATE:fi-collar]) for Amazon Associate links.
  • Campaign Tracking: Coordinated pushes are tracked via unique Amazon ascsubtags for accurate ROI reporting.
